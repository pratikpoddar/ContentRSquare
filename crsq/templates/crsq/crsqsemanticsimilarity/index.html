CRSQ Semantic Similarity

See how closely two elements of a pair are related. Semantic similarity or semantic relatedness is a metric defined over a set of terms, where the idea of distance between them is based on the likeness of their meaning or semantic content as opposed to similarity which can be estimated regarding their syntactical representation.
Example: (Sachin Tendulkar / Rahul Dravid), (Sachin Tendulkar / Rahul Gandhi), (Rahul Gandhi / Narendra Modi), (love / romance), (love / cricket). Give it a shot. We are improving with time by adding more knowledge into the system. This is just the beginning.
