Sixteen-year-old boy charged with murder over use of Playstation in Chile
2010-04-29
Chile, April 29, 2010, Published, Archived, South America, Crime and law, Science and technology, Santiago, Chile, 
A sixteen-year-old boy from Santiago, Chile has been charged with the murder of his brother after the pair got in a fight over whose turn it was to use a PlayStation 3. Police reported that the sixteen-year-old had stabbed his eighteen-year-old brother after he turned the PlayStation on without his younger brother&rsquo;s permission.It's reported that the younger brother entered the kitchen and took a knife; he then stabbed his brother in the chest. Chile police reported that the youth said that &quot;things had got out of control&quot;. If convicted, the boy would stay five years in prison.At the time of the death there was no one else in the building. The boy&rsquo;s birth mother had abandoned them ten years ago and they had been adopted by a Belgian woman. At the time of the attack, she was visiting her home country.
