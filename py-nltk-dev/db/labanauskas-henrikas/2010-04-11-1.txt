Rodeo: Crist&oacute;bal Cortina and V&iacute;ctor Vergara win Chile Championship
2010-04-11
Chile, April 11, 2010, Translated news, Published, Archived, Sports, South America, Rancagua, O'Higgins Region, 
Rancagua, Chile &ndash; Crist&oacute;bal Cortina and V&iacute;ctor Vergara, in conjunction with their horses &quot;Cumpa&quot; and &quot;T&iacute;o Pedro&quot;, have won the last phase in the 62nd Campeonato Nacional de Rodeo. The Sello de Raza was given to &quot;Fant&aacute;stico&quot;, from the Santa Isabel Farm and mounted by Eduardo Tamayo &Oacute;rdenes.This championship concludes the Chilean rodeo 2009&ndash;2010 season and was performed at the Medialuna in Rancagua.
